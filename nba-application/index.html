The Game

BASKETBALL
IS MY DNA

"I don't just understand basketball.
I speak it. I live it."

I picked up a basketball as a kid in Egypt and never put it down. I played at national youth level, competed across the Middle East and Europe, and have spent years coaching community and youth teams. This isn't a weekend hobby. This is a lifelong relationship with a sport that shaped how I see the world.

I've watched the NBA evolve from the Jordan era to the global entertainment phenomenon it is today. I've followed EuroLeague, the G-League, FIBA, and grassroots scenes on five continents. I understand what fans feel at every touchpoint — from the electricity of a Game 7 crowd to the quiet magic of a pickup game in a neighborhood park. And that understanding is the foundation of every activation, fan experience, and public event I've ever designed.

That's what makes me different. I'm not a great events professional who happens to like basketball. I'm the basketball person who spent 27 years building fan experiences, brand activations, and public events at global scale — and I know exactly what it feels like to be in the crowd. The NBA's fans deserve experiences designed by someone who feels the game the way they do.

Play 01

The NBA's international expansion is the biggest opportunity in global sports. Play 02
Public Events That Convert Casual Viewers to Lifelong Fans

My biggest events — Dubai's Marina Music Festival, Street Nights, COP activations — weren't just logistically impressive. They pulled people in and made them feel something. Play 03
Entertainment-First Thinking at Every Touchpoint

Basketball is entertainment. The best NBA experiences aren't just games — they're shows. I'd bring an entertainment-first lens to every activation: the pre-game energy, halftime experience, around-the-arena culture, the digital amplification. Not safe and corporate. Creative, bold, and built around the moment.

The courts where I played and coached are where the next generation of NBA fans are born. Grassroots basketball activation — clinics, leagues, community events tied to NBA moments — creates deeper loyalty than any marketing campaign. I've lived on those courts. I know how to build the bridge from the community game to the global stage.
