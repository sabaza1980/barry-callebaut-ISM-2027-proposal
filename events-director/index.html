How I work

Led by curiosity, delivered with discipline.

Every event begins with a question: how do we want people to feel? Emotion is what makes an experience memorable — so that's where the design starts. Curiosity drives the creative, and two decades of operational rigour make sure the feeling survives contact with reality. I own the full lifecycle: concept, production, budget and P&L, stakeholder programming, and measurement.
