Project Overview

Setting the Stage for ISM 2027

Barry Callebaut will once again be present at ISM Cologne, welcoming 90–100 colleagues and key customers. This proposal outlines a comprehensive agency partnership to manage the full attendee logistics journey — from hotel coordination to booth party registrations — powered by a purpose-built digital platform and a dedicated on-site coordinator.

01

Digital Platform

A dedicated web platform repurposed from Innovation Summit, tailored for ISM. Attendees manage their own hotel bookings; the team tracks rooming lists in real time.

02

Hotel Logistics

Seamless coordination of room blocks across multiple hotels for 90–100 BC delegates, with automated confirmations, amendments, and final rooming list delivery.

03

Booth Party

A complete RSVP module for the BC booth event — enabling the sales team to invite customers, guests to confirm attendance, and post-event comms to close the loop.

Scope of Work

What We'll Deliver

🖥

A — Platform Repurposing & Technical Development

Adapt the Innovation Summit platform for ISM Cologne 2027
  • Repurpose and rebrand the existing platform for ISM Cologne 2027
  • Integrate hotel room blocks provided by BC Procurement
  • Build attendee-facing booking portal for self-service room reservations
  • Automated rooming list generation for each hotel
  • Admin dashboard for the BC logistics team to monitor bookings
  • Booking amendment and cancellation workflows
  • IT liaison with BC IT Team for DNS, DKIM and SPF configuration
  • Transactional email setup: confirmations, reminders, amendments
  • RSVP & registration module for booth party guests
  • Sales team–facing invite redirect to guest registration page
  • Automated guest confirmation, reminder (×2), and post-event thank-you emails
  • Guest attendance tracking and export
📬

B — Email Infrastructure Setup

Safe, branded email delivery for all platform communications
  • Coordinate with BC IT to obtain DNS, DKIM and SPF values
  • Configure sending domain for transactional email
  • Ensure inbound reply handling is correctly routed
  • Test delivery across major email clients and flag any compliance issues
🗂

C — Logistics Coordinator (June 2026 – January 2027)

Dedicated agency resource managing day-to-day coordination
  • Serve as the primary point of contact between BC and hotels
  • Manage individual attendee bookings, changes and special requests
  • Chase outstanding room confirmations and maintain accuracy of rooming lists
  • Coordinate hotel cut-off dates and room release schedules
  • Liaise with BC Procurement on hotel contract milestones
  • Manage booth party guest list: confirmations, dietary needs, capacity
  • Issue final rooming lists to each hotel in required formats
  • On-site support in Cologne during the event (January 2027)
  • Provide post-event reconciliation and reporting

Booth Party Module

Customer Invitation & RSVP Journey

The booth party module enables the BC sales team to invite customers via personalised email links. Guests are directed to a branded registration page, receive automated confirmations and reminders, and the team has full visibility of attendance in real time.

4
🎉

Automated thank-you email sent to all attendees after the event — closing the loop and reinforcing the BC relationship.
